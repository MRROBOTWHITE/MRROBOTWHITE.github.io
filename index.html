A little bit about me

I'm a normal teenager who is seriously studying cybersecurity for me it's just fun. I like to find vulnerabilities in websites, programs, cameras, etc. I do not use information to anyone's detriment and I do not advise you to do it, for me it is like a hobby. I can spend days in front of computer creating web sites, bots for different tasks or looking for vulnerabilities in systems

I'm all for white hacking, if you find a vulnerability in a site or program report it to the developer. It's not the system that is vulnerable, it's the person who made the mistake in creating it. We are all human, and it's okay to make mistakes. Be for white hacking with a clear conscience.
